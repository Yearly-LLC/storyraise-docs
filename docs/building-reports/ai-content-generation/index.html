AI content generation

Storyraise has AI built in to help with the hardest part of any report: the blank page. Use it to draft whole sections and to generate imagery — then make the results your own.

Draft a section with AI

The Describe & Draft a New Section tool builds entire report sections from a description:

  1. Click Add new section and choose the AI drafting option.
  2. Describe what the section is about — for example, "Our youth mentorship program matched 120 students with mentors this year, with 94% reporting improved confidence." Or pick one of the suggested ideas to start.
  3. Click generate. Storyraise outlines and drafts the section for you.
  4. Edit the result like any other section — adjust the words, swap images, restyle.

Getting good drafts

  • Give it your numbers and names. "We served 3,400 meals at our downtown shelter" produces a far better draft than "write about our food program."
  • One section per ask. Describe a single topic; build the report section by section.
  • Treat it as a first draft. The AI gets you from blank to rough; your voice and your details make it real. Always review before publishing.

Generate images with AI

Where you add media, you can also generate it: describe the image you want and Storyraise creates it. Useful for illustrative and decorative imagery when you don't have a photo that fits.

A word of advice: for impact storytelling, real photos of your real work will always beat generated images. Use AI imagery for abstract or decorative spots, not in place of authentic photography.

What AI can see

The AI works from what you type in your prompt and the context of your report. Your constituent data is not used to train AI models.
