# Setting up your account

Getting started on Storyraise takes a couple of minutes. This guide walks you from the sign-up screen to your new dashboard.

## Create your account

Go to the sign-up page and fill in **Create your Storyraise account**:

- **Organization Name** — your nonprofit's name, e.g. *The SR Foundation*.
- **Storyraise URL** — the web address your published reports will live under. Storyraise suggests one based on your organization name (like `sr.storyraise.com`), and you can edit it. This becomes your reports' home: `https://your-organization.yearly.report`.
- **Full Name** — your name.
- **Title** — your role (optional).
- **Work Email** — the address you'll sign in with.
- **Create a Password** — use the show/hide toggle to check it.

Agree to the Terms & Conditions and create your account. Prefer Google? Use **Sign up with Google** to skip the password step.

## Confirm your URL

After the first step, you'll get one last chance to fine-tune your **Storyraise URL** — *Awesome! One last thing.* Choose carefully: this subdomain appears in every report link you share, so pick something short and recognizable (your organization's name or acronym works well).

## What happens next

You land on your dashboard, ready to build. A strong first hour:

1. **Set up your brand kit** — add your logo, colors, and fonts so every report starts on brand. See [Brand kit](../building-reports/brand-kit.md).
2. **Create your first report** — start from a template. See [Creating your first report](../getting-started/creating-your-first-report.md).
3. **Invite your team** — bring colleagues into your organization. See [Managing your team](managing-your-team.md).
4. **Import your constituents** — connect a CRM or upload a CSV to unlock personalization. See [Connecting a CRM](../crm-and-data/connecting-a-crm.md).

## Signing in later

Return anytime at [app.storyraise.com](https://app.storyraise.com). Forgot your password? See [Your profile and security](profile-and-security.md) for the reset steps.
