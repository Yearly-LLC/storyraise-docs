# Organization settings

Your organization is the shared workspace that holds your reports, constituents, brand kit, and team. This page covers the organization-level settings in your account area.

## Your organization info

Under **Your Org Info** you'll find:

- **Organization Name** — how your organization is identified across Storyraise.
- **Custom URL** — your reports' web address, `your-organization.yearly.report`.

These are shown for reference. To change them, contact support — your URL in particular is woven into every published report link, so it isn't edited casually.

## Your brand

Most of what makes your organization *look* like your organization lives in the **brand kit** — logos, colors, and primary/secondary fonts that apply across every report. See [Brand kit](../building-reports/brand-kit.md).

## Nonprofit certificate

Organization admins can upload your nonprofit certificate under **Nonprofit Certificate** (**Add docs** — PDF or DOC). This verifies your nonprofit status with Storyraise. Upload it once; you can replace or remove it later.

## Sending email from your domain

To send email from your own domain (so messages come from `you@yourdomain.org` rather than a Storyraise address), set up a custom sending domain. See [Setting up a custom sending domain](custom-sending-domain.md).

## Belonging to more than one organization

If you work with multiple organizations on Storyraise — say, a foundation and a fiscally sponsored project — you can belong to several at once. Use **Find account** to search your organizations and switch between them; each has its own reports, constituents, and settings.
