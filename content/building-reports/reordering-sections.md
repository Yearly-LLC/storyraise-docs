# Reordering sections

The order of your sections is the arc of your story. Storyraise makes it easy to rearrange sections as your report takes shape.

## Reorder by drag and drop

1. Open the section sorter — the view that shows all your sections as a list with drag handles.
2. Drag a section by its handle to its new position.
3. The report's reading order and navigation menu update to match.

## Order changes everything else

Reordering updates the report's **table of contents**, which drives the reader-facing navigation menu — there's nothing to fix up manually after a move. See [Navigation options](navigation-options.md).

## Thinking about order

A structure that works for most impact reports:

1. **Hook** — an Intro section with your strongest image and a clear theme
2. **Credibility** — a Letter from leadership
3. **Proof** — Stories, Stats, and Galleries, alternating narrative and visual sections
4. **Gratitude** — Testimonials and your Donor List
5. **Ask** — a Donate section while the impact is fresh

Two practical tips:

- **Put your best story early.** Many readers won't reach the end; don't save the strongest material for last.
- **End with the ask.** The Donate section belongs after the emotional peak, not before it.
